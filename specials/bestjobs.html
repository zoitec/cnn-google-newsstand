There's a new no. 1 best job in America: Mobile App Developer. And while techies took the top spot, there are Best Jobs in all kinds of fields - from health care to finance, to education, real estate and even mining, For this exclusive list, CNNMoney partnered with Payscale.com to find the top 100 carerrs with big growth, great pay and satisfying work. Also considered: how meaningful or stressful a job is, how difficult it is to get started in a given field, the likelihood of promotions, and, where possible, the ability to telecommute.
